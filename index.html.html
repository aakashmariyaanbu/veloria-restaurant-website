Our Heritage

A Story
of Passion
& Craft

🌍

Born from a love of travel

Veloria was founded in 2016 by Chef Isabelle Moreau and restaurateur David Nakamura — two lifelong wanderers who believed that the world's greatest cuisines deserved a single, extraordinary home in London.

After a decade of travel across 60 countries, tasting street food in Tokyo, feasting in Marrakech, and training in the finest kitchens of Lyon, they returned to London with a singular vision: to bring the world to your table.

Today, Veloria stands as London's premier multi-cuisine destination, awarded 7 Michelin stars across our cuisine journeys and beloved by guests from over 40 nationalities.

🏆

Recognition and excellence

Our journey has been one of relentless pursuit of perfection. From sourcing ingredients directly from Japanese fish markets, to maintaining relationships with Italian truffle hunters and Kashmiri spice merchants.

We do not simply cook food from around the world — we honour it. Each cuisine is led by a chef born and raised in that culinary tradition, ensuring absolute authenticity in every dish we serve.

Meet the Team

👩‍🍳
Chef Isabelle Moreau
Co-Founder & Executive Chef
Trained at Le Cordon Bleu, Paris. 20 years across the finest kitchens of Lyon, Kyoto and New York. Isabelle leads our French and Nordic kitchen with uncompromising precision.
🧑‍🍳
Chef Rajan Mehta
Head of Indian & Middle Eastern
Born in Jaipur, Rajan trained under legendary masters in New Delhi before honing his craft in Dubai's finest hotels. His spice knowledge is encyclopaedic.
👨‍🍳
Chef Kenji Watanabe
Head of Japanese & Asian Cuisine
A third-generation sushi master from Osaka. Kenji brings 25 years of omakase artistry to Veloria, having trained at the prestigious Sukiyabashi Jiro in Tokyo.
